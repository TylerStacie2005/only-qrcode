# Privacy Policy - Only QR Code

**Last updated: March 1, 2026**

## Overview

Only QR Code does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Period.

## Data Collection

We collect **nothing**. Specifically:

- No personal information
- No usage analytics
- No crash reports
- No device identifiers
- No location data
- No cookies or tracking pixels

## Data Storage

All QR codes you generate are created locally on your device. No data is sent to any server.

## Third-Party Services

Only QR Code does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or analytics tools.

## Internet Access

Only QR Code does not require an internet connection to function. The app works entirely offline.

## Children's Privacy

Only QR Code does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this policy, we will update this page. But honestly, there's nothing to change — we don't collect data and we never will.
